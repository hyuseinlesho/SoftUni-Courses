World Cup News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA WORLD Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorder on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.

This legendary match also brought global renown for Archile Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an invidual player in single international match.

And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
